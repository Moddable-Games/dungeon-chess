How It Works

I

Pick Your Map

Compact arena or sprawling dungeon cross. Each layout demands different tactics.

II

Choose Your Faction

Each faction has unique units with abilities that change how you approach the board.

III

Draft Your Team

Spend your 75 XP budget wisely. Mix heavy-hitters with cheap fodder or go all-in on elites.

IV

Deploy Your Forces

Place your pieces in your spawn zone. Positioning matters before the first move.

V

Battle

Turn-based combat. Capture the enemy king. Use terrain, abilities, and cunning to win.

Features

Animated Dungeon Board

Stone textures, flickering torchlight, floating embers, and water caustics bring the dungeon to life.

Unique Unit Abilities

Every piece has special rules. Archers attack across gaps. Fire Elementals can't cross water. Knights leap walls.

Modular Map System

26 board sections create endless combinations. 2-player skirmishes or 4-player free-for-alls.

AI Opponent

A heuristic AI that respects all unit abilities, terrain constraints, and draft rules. Challenging from your first game.
